Camping Tips

Tip 1: Choose the Right Spot

Always choose a flat and dry area for your campsite. Avoid low-lying areas where water can accumulate during rain.

Tip 2: Pack Smart

Pack light but don't forget essentials like a first-aid kit, a flashlight, and enough water and food for the trip.

Tip 3: Stay Safe

Keep your campsite clean to avoid attracting wildlife. Always follow safety protocols when using a campfire.

Viral Location: Camp Lu Ying, Bukit Tinggi

Camp Lu Ying, set in the picturesque Bukit Tinggi in Pahang, offers a unique glamping and camping experience. Known as Camp Eagle, it is a paradise for families seeking adventure coupled with comfort. Location & Environment: The campsite is situated in a scenic setting, offering breathtaking views and a tranquil atmosphere. Its accessibility and variety of camping environments, including hill scenery, enhance its appeal. Facilities & Activities: From various camping zones with different pricing options to luxurious glamping, Camp Lu Ying caters to all. Modern washrooms, a natural spring water pool, and a cafe add to the experience.
